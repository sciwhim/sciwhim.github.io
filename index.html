Welcome to Sciwhim...


$ Each of us is passionate about something. We don’t seek motivation or inspiration to work on it. We are forever inspired, deeply focused, self-motivated and it’s immutably strong every day, at any weather, any mood or location.

$ Each of us is working on something different, to learn, explore, to bring it to perfection, to invent.

$ Ever had this feeling, when you are not sure if you can accomplish something, but still go for it?
We try and fail not because we weren’t thorough enough, but because we have challenging goals. We try again, and fail again. And again. Until we succeed.

$ Sometimes there is a wild urge to talk to someone about something technical we’re doing, to make sure we aren’t crazy in what we are trying to accomplish, or to ask an opinion about some technical aspect we’re about to implement, or to get a different perspective, or … to just share the progress. Often, solutions to hardest problems find you when you are trying to explain the problem to someone else and just thinking aloud.

$ This is not about supervision, tutorials, talks, workshops, or other educational events - you can watch video lessons and read books or papers on your own. This is about being yourself and being around people who are fond of working on a thing they are passionate about and finding similar, yet different minds.

$ That’s why Sciwhim exists.
